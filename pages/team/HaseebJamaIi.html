MUHAMMAD HASEEB JAMALI

Partner – S. A. Jamali & Co. Karachi

Muhammad Haseeb Jamali is a resilient and invincible lawyer. He is popular with the bar and bench alike owing to his irreproachable, upright and credible nature. Haseeb is ruthless in the courts and thorough with his case preparation – admired by both his clients and opponents, who value him for his principled stance and eloquent speech.

Haseeb obtained his Master’s degree in Civil and Commercial Law from University College London (UCL), London in the year 2005. He was a double gold medalist and first class first position holder during his Bachelor’s in Law from Hamdard Law School. Moreover, he also secured second position in Bachelor’s in Commerce from Sindh University, Hyderabad.

As a Partner at M/s Abid S. Zuberi & Co., Haseeb enjoyed a diverse clientele for a period of 13 years including many high-profile companies, private organizations and distinguished politicians. However, now Haseeb has his own established independent practice as part of S. A. Jamali & Co. where he is a Senior Partner. Needless to say, Haseeb brings to the office a treasure trove of experience, knowledge and expertise.

LITIGATION EXPERIENCE

Haseeb Jamali has over 18 years’ of standing in the Courts as an active litigator with considerable expertise in the fields of Constitutional, Civil, Family, Property and Election Law. His diverse practice also revolves around areas of Criminal, Banking, Employment, Taxation, National Accountability Bureau, Societies, Trade Mark and Admiralty Law where he has not only attained favourable results but has also carved a name and reputation for himself.

With Haseeb’s legal acumen, profound understanding of technical issues, and his ability to prepare and plead a case decisively, whole-heartedly and convincingly, he is considered a specialist on bail matters and cases of urgent nature such as stay orders. He is often engaged by clients on urgent and short notice but has seldom failed them. He has numerous reported judgments to his credit.

Haseeb is an advocate of the High Court of Sindh since 2006 and advocate Supreme Court of Pakistan since 2016.

OTHER EXPERIENCE

Haseeb is a hard-core litigator. However, one of his other inclinations has been teaching as a lecturer at the Hamdard School of Law.
