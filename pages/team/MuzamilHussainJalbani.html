Muzamil Hussain Jalbani

SENIOR ASSOCIATES
  • Advocate High Court
  • LLB – University of Karachi (S.M Law College)
  • Senior Associate – S.A. Jamali & Co

Muzamil Hussain Jalbani is Senior Associate at S.A.Jamali & Co. having 7 years of legal experience. He joined S.A.Jamali & Co. in January 2020 and assists Senior Partner, Mr. Muhammad Haseeb Jamali ASC.

Information

He acquired a Bachelor of Arts Degree from Shah Abdul Lateef University Khairpur and he completed his LLB in 2018 from University of Karachi through affiliated college (Sindh Muslim Law College Karachi)

He has worked on all facets of law, including matters pertaining to Constitutional, Civil, Criminal, Family, Land, Rent Law, and NAB matters. He has pleaded and assisted on cases at the High Court, Subordinate Courts, and Special Courts.

Mr. Jalbani has been instrumental in assisting his senior, Mr. Muhammad Haseeb Jamali, in handling high-profile cases before the Supreme Court and High Courts. His keen legal acumen and dedication to achieve the best possible outcomes for clients have been invaluable in complex legal matters. Mr. Jalbani's ability to meticulously analyze legal issues, conduct thorough research, and present compelling arguments has earned him the trust and respect of both his colleagues and the legal community.

Notably, Mr. Jalbani was appointed as Amicus Curiae by the Hon'ble High Court of Sindh, where he provided valuable assistance to the High Court in applicability of the Cooperative Society Act 1925/2020 and it's purpose.

Through his diligent efforts and strong work ethic, Mr. Jalbani has achieved a significant milestone by reporting cases in a remarkably short span of time. His hard work has earned him recognition, and he continues to excel in his profession. Several of his notable cases have been reported, demonstrating his expertise and dedication to the field.

He also renders assistance in legal research, drafting Suits & Agreements, and other necessary legal documentation.
