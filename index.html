Justice Anwar Zaheer Jamali

Chairman – Former Chief Justice of Pakistan

Justice Anwar Zaheer Jamali served as the 24th Chief Justice of Pakistan from 2015 to 2016. With decades of service in the judiciary, including his tenure as Chief Justice of the Sindh High Court and Acting Chief Election Commissioner, he is widely respected for his integrity and commitment to justice.

He is best known for his unwavering stance during the 2007 judicial crisis, where he refused to take oath under an unconstitutional regime, showcasing his dedication to the Constitution and rule of law. Today, his wisdom and leadership guide our firm toward ethical practices, fairness, and strategic vision.
